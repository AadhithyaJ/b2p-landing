Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2025

1. Who We Are

B2P Teachers ("we", "our", "us") provides live online coaching for school students across Tamil Nadu. Our website is b2pteachers.com and our registration platform is app.b2pteachers.com. For any privacy queries, contact us at b2pteachers@gmail.com or call +91 70946 75153.

2. Information We Collect

When you register or make a payment, we collect:

  • Student details — name, class/grade, school name
  • Parent/Guardian details — name, mobile number, email address
  • Payment information — processed securely by Razorpay; we do not store card or UPI credentials
  • Usage data — pages visited, browser type, device, and IP address (via standard web analytics)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process enrollment and send class access details
  • To send attendance reports, progress updates, and reminders via WhatsApp or SMS
  • To respond to support queries
  • To improve our classes and website experience
  • To send promotional messages about new courses or offers (you may opt out at any time)

4. Children's Privacy

Our services are designed for school-age children. We collect student information only with parental/guardian consent obtained during registration. We do not knowingly allow children to register independently and we do not share student data with third parties for advertising purposes.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Razorpay — to process payments securely
  • WhatsApp Business API — to send class updates and notifications
  • Google Analytics / Meta Pixel — anonymized usage analytics only
  • Legal authorities if required by law

6. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences and measure ad performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; note that some site features may not function correctly if you do so.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your child is enrolled with B2P Teachers and for up to 2 years after the last interaction, after which it is securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, email us at b2pteachers@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Security

We use HTTPS encryption and industry-standard security practices to protect your data. Payment transactions are handled entirely by Razorpay's PCI-DSS-compliant infrastructure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
